SPORT TEAMS

Tampa Bay Buccaneers (NFL)

The Buccaneers are a professional football team based in Tampa Bay. They have won multiple championships and are known for their passionate fans.

Tampa Bay Lightning (NHL)

The Lightning are a professional hockey team based in Tampa Bay. They have won multiple championships and play in the state-of-the-art Amalie Arena.

Tampa Bay Rays (MLB)

The Rays are a professional baseball team based in St. Petersburg, just across the bay from Tampa. They have made several playoff appearances and play in Tropicana Field.

UNIVERSITIES

University of South Florida

USF is a public research university with multiple campuses in Tampa Bay area and known forthe programs in business, engineering, and medicine.

Hillsborough Community College

HCC is a community college with several campuses in the Tampa Bay area. It offers a wide range of programs and is a popular choice for local students.

University of Tampa

UT is a private university located in the heart of downtown Tampa. It is known for its strong programs in business, communication, and the arts.
